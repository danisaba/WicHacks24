What is Proxima?

Meet up with your friends. With this app, you can meet up with nearby friends through a proximity feature that will send you a notification if you are nearby someone who has started an event. Have fun, meet new people, and hangout around your campus, city, and more!

Getting ignored in the groupchat?

Not anymore! Proxima is an app that helps connect people around campus by letting others know where they are, what they are doing, and what they are looking to do. This app increases people's interaction opportunities by making their location and availability more visible and easy to interact with!

Proxima is inspired by the need to make it easy to meet up with friends and colleagues who are available and doing nothing. It is based on Proxima Centauri, which is the closest star to our own Sun! This app, like the stars, tries to find people close to you.
